## Problem

High Ground Coffee Club has no e-commerce site. Needs a storefront that can sell recurring coffee subscriptions (not just one-off products) — recurring billing, pause/skip/cancel, and plan customization are core to the business, not add-ons.

## Target User

- Coffee enthusiasts who want curated/rotating coffee delivered on a schedule
- Gift buyers purchasing a subscription for someone else
- Site owner (admin) managing catalog, subscriptions, and fulfillment

## Goals

- Launch a subscription-first Shopify storefront
- Recurring billing with self-service pause/skip/cancel
- Plan customization: brew method, roast/grind, quantity, frequency
- One-time gift subscriptions (gift note, scheduled delivery)
- UX quality on par with reference site (atlascoffeeclub.com)

## Core Features

*Benchmarked against atlascoffeeclub.com — see Technical Reference for full notes.*

- Subscription plan builder (brew method: drip, pods, French press, espresso; decaf option)
- Recurring billing app (Recharge / Skio / Bold — TBD, see Open Questions)
- Self-service pause/skip/cancel in customer account
- Gift subscriptions (mail or email delivery, gift note, scheduled send date)
- Coffee catalog with origin story / tasting notes
- Educational content: how-it-works, brew guides
- Free shipping + money-back guarantee messaging
- Account login, FAQ, About, Contact, legal pages (Terms, Privacy, Cookie, Accessibility)

## Constraints

- Platform: Shopify (decided — see Decisions Log in Project Overview)
- Coffee catalog not yet provided — pending next session
- Standard (non-promotional) pricing tiers not yet defined
- Claude Code can write theme/storefront code but cannot perform Shopify admin actions (store creation, app installs, payment/tax config) without the user providing access — those remain manual, user-owned steps

## Open Questions

- Subscription pricing tiers and delivery frequency options (weekly/biweekly/monthly?)
- Which Shopify subscription app to use (Recharge vs Skio vs Bold vs native Shopify Subscriptions API)
- Bag size(s) offered (reference site uses 12 oz)
- Rotating single-origin model (like reference) vs fixed core catalog?
- Gifting scope for v1: full gift-note/email-delivery flow, or simpler bundle?
- Is a community/rewards feature (reference has one on a subdomain) in scope, or later phase?
- New Shopify store or existing one? Who holds credentials/access?
