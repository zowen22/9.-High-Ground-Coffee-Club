## Summary

Brown Bag Coffee Club is the coffee subscription service brand of High Ground Coffee (the parent company), benchmarked against Atlas Coffee Club (atlascoffeeclub.com). It sells recurring coffee deliveries with brew-method customization, gifting, and educational content, built on Shopify.

**Brand architecture:** High Ground Coffee = parent company. Brown Bag Coffee Club = the subscription product/storefront brand customers see (site name, Shopify store name, marketing). This mirrors how Atlas Coffee Club itself is branded as a standalone club rather than under its parent's corporate name.
